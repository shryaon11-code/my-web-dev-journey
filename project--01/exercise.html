Get ready to crush your limits! Check out our routines for all levels.

Workout Routines:

  • Beginner Routine: 3 days/week – Basic strength and form
  • Intermediate Routine: 4–5 days/week – Mix of strength & cardio
  • Advanced Routine: 5–6 days/week – Muscle-building, high intensity

Exercise Details:

1. Push-Ups

  • Target Muscles: Chest, Triceps, Shoulders, Core
  • How to Do (Step-by-step):
    1. Place hands shoulder-width apart on the floor.
    2. Keep your body straight from head to heels.
    3. Lower chest close to the floor.
    4. Push back up to starting position.
  • Tips: Keep core tight, elbows at 45°, don’t let hips sag.

2. Squats

  • Target Muscles: Quadriceps, Glutes, Hamstrings, Core
  • How to Do:
    1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
    2. Rest barbell across shoulders or do bodyweight version.
    3. Lower hips until thighs are parallel to the floor.
    4. Push back up through heels.
  • Tips: Keep knees behind toes, back straight, and chest lifted.

3. Deadlifts

  • Target Muscles: Lower Back, Hamstrings, Glutes, Core
  • How to Do:
    1. Stand with feet hip-width apart, barbell on the floor.
    2. Bend hips and knees, grip the bar shoulder-width apart.
    3. Keep back straight, chest up, lift bar to standing.
    4. Lower the bar slowly back to the ground.
  • Tips: Engage core, lift with legs not back, avoid rounding spine.

4. Dumbbell Bicep Curls

  • Target Muscles: Biceps
  • How to Do:
    1. Stand tall holding dumbbells at your sides.
    2. Curl the dumbbells upward toward shoulders.
    3. Pause briefly at the top.
    4. Lower slowly to starting position.
  • Tips: Keep elbows close to torso, avoid swinging arms, control movement.
